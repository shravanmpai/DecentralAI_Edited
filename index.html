Overview

D Exchange AI is a pioneering project aimed at revolutionizing the way data and AI models are shared and utilized. By leveraging blockchain technology and advanced Al capabilities, our platform creates a marketplace that fosters collaboration, enhances transparency, and ensures fair compensation. Our mission is to break down the barriers of centralized data control and unlock the true potential of Al for everyone.

FEATURES OF THE PLATFORM

01

Data Validation with Great Expectations

The platform utilizes Great Expectations, a data validation library, to define and enforce expectations on datasets.

02

Integration with Google Drive

The platform seamlessly integrates with Google Drive, allowing users to upload validated datasets for storage and collaboration.

03

Generation and Storage of Validation Reports

After validating the dataset, the platform generates detailed validation reports.

04

Upload to IPFS for Immutability

a decentralized and distributed file system, to ensure the immutability and tamper resistance of validation reports.

05

Smart Contract Deployment

Smart contracts are deployed on the blockchain to store metadata associated with validation reports.
